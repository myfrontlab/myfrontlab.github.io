About us

The augmented reality platform
for galleries, artists and events

ARtissimo allows artists and galleries to extend the borders of their art by using augmented reality. The augmented reality layer lets us and our viewers get into a new depth of impression. This new world can be unlocked by any smartphone or tablet and offer WOW experience.

Two parts of our solution:

ARtissimo — the builder that can enrich your artwork by creating new dimension for its exhibition

Aggregatore — the event aggregator with AR support for shows, museums and art spaces

Builder

ARtissimo

ARtissimo is a builder that will help you enrich your masterpieces with WOW effect.

We make sure that literally in an hour anyone could make their own incredible installation art.

In addition to the standard overlay of an image or video over a mark, we also offer features such as:

  • Event system
  • Interaction
  • And other intriguing features...
AGgregatore

Aggregatore is what will help
your viewers find you.

In fact, it is an aggregator of galleries, exhibitions and art spaces with GEO location and purchase of tickets.

Aggregatore also can display AR content and redirect users to ticket sites.

Aggregatore will be available on Google Play and AppStore.
