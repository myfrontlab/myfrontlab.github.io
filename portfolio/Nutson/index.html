What is NUTSon?
NUTSon is a short video platform where users can be rewarded for social activity, watching and creating content with blockchain technology

Watch-To-Earn

Earn money by watching other users' videos, give shakas,
reposting and other in-app activities

Create-To-Earn

Create content and earn rewards for shakas (analogue
of likes), reposts and views

Challenge rewards

Any user can create a challenge or take part and win
rewards for winning the challenge

Likes and donation

Viewers can easily and conveniently support you not
only by liking and commenting, but also by donating

The platform
Our iOS, Android or web platform provides easy-to-use tools for watching and creating content. Take your videos to the next level with our app and get a new level of user experience.
The Economy
With built-in mechanics NUTSon allows some users to get new emotions and others to earn without wasting time recruiting an audience. All of this is made possible by a detailed economic.

Tokenomics, vesting
shedule & utility
We have created NTS as a governance token of our platform. NTN is a utility stablecoin, you get them as reward inside the app.
Each can be obtained by exchanging in the app's built-in wallet. Users earn NTN simply by watching their favorite videos in the feed, same thing as before, but now they get money in the form of tokens, which give more freedom and openness to monetization than ever before!
